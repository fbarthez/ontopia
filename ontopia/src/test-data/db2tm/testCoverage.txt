This document shows for which purpose each test case was created, and gives a summary of how much of db2tm that has been tested so far.

No relations:
  | norelations.xml
Single relation:
  | missingcsv.xml
  | onerelation.xml
  | onerelation-multi-use.xml
  | onerelation-multi-use2.xml
Multiple relations:
  | multiple-relations.xml
  | multiple-relations2.xml
  | onecsv-multiple-relations.xml
Various attributes on the <csv> tag:
  | csvTag.xml
  | csvTag-utf-8.xml
 
ERROR CASES:
Repeated relations with same name (csv):
  | onecsv-multiple-relations.ltm
Missing csv file:
  | missingcsv.xml (now in bugs. Bug 
#1829)
Referencing a column that doesn't exist:
  | illegalFooBar.xml
 
Dette gir 42% line-coverage og 92% 
branch-coverage (jmf. jcoverage 
rapport), ekskludert tre classer som 
ikke skal testes (Execute, 
JDBCDataSource og WebAppUtils).
 
Mestparten av de utestede kodelinjene i 
Processor er støtte for remove, og 
trenger ikke å testes. Mestparten av de 
utestede kodelinjene i RelationMapping 
brukes heller ikke, og trenger heller 
ikke å testes. Da disse er de desidert 
største klassene i DB2TM, regner jeg 
med at minst 70% av de relevante 
kodelinjene er testet.
